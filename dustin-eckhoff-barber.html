BIO

Hi, I'm Dustin, and I've been a barber for over 15 years. I recently relocated back to Tucson after managing the #1 rated barbershop in Phoenix for the past 4 years. I've had the opportunity to work with barbers from different parts of the world who have helped me master the latest techniques for the most popular styles. So whether you are more traditional or adventurous, whether you want to go long or short, I will work with you to create your unique style, and want to come back again and again.
